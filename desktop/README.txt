SPLINTERLANDS: RIFT SIEGE

A fan-made tower defence prototype inspired by Splinterlands.

HOW TO PLAY
Double-click "Splinterlands-Rift-Siege-1.0.0-Portable.exe".
No installation or browser is required.

CONTROLS
- Select a small card icon, then place it on a map rune.
- Click a placed tower to evolve, sell, or change targeting.
- Space pauses the battle.
- T changes targeting for the selected tower.
- Use the arrow on the right to open campaign intel and the Bestiary.

ATTRIBUTION
Inspired by Splinterlands. Splinterlands names, card references, and artwork
are used with the project team's stated permission for this prototype.
This fan-made concept is not presented as an official Splinterlands release.
